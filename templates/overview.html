Why use Goal Keeper?

Why set goals with Goal Keeper?

Clearer Focus
Properly thought out and stated goals clearly set out your intentions and desires; the things you really want to achieve.

Peace of mind
Too often you can have many things on your mind. Writing down your goals can help you take all of those ideas, apply perspective and priority, then galvanize you into commitment and action.

Measure your progress
Setting goals allows you to measure how well effectively you are moving towards achieving them.

Get Started Today
⇣
1
Goals
Simply select "Add Goal" in the "Goals" secction to add your first goal today!
2
Add Category
Goals are divided into different categories to help you manage them. If you want to add another category, simply select “Add Category” from the "Categories" section. This category will then be available for you to add goals to.
3
Add Steps
You can also add steps to your goals that will help you to break down your goals into manageable and trackable stages. This can be done by viewing your individual goals in the "Goals" section and choosing to “Add Step”.
4
Check your Progress
Once you have started adding goals, you can check your progress anytime by selecting “Check your Progress” section.
